## What is a Dedicated Development Team and Its Role?

A dedicated development team is a group of IT specialists (developers, designers, project managers, QA testers) who work on a project for a long or focused period to achieve the main objectives of that project.

The role of a dedicated development team is usually to work with a company towards specific goals and targets. They engage in numerous activities such as mobile app development, software product development, cloud solutions, and many more.

## Dedicated Development Team vs. Fixed Price Model

Think about all the things that might affect the success of your project before you choose the engagement model. Several important selection criteria, including cost, quality, flexibility, and time management, will be considered when we evaluate the two methods here.

### Dedicated Development Team

- **Flexibility:** Highly flexible, adapting to changing needs and markets.
- **Cost:** Higher upfront costs, but cost-effective in the long run.
- **Quality:** High-quality, custom work due to continuous involvement.
- **Timeline:** Flexible with timelines, great for complex projects.
- **Scalability:** Easily scalable based on project needs.
- **Risk Management:** Risk is shared between client and provider.
- **IP Control:** Continuous control over IP throughout the project.

### Fixed Price Model

- **Flexibility:** Limited scope, changes may extend the timeline or cost.
- **Cost:** Defined budget, ideal for fixed-scope projects.
- **Quality:** Quality may vary, as the focus is on timeline and budget.
- **Timeline:** Strict timelines are ideal for short-term.
- **Client Involvement:** Limited client involvement, with updates based on initial requirements.
- **Scalability:** Limited scalability, requiring re-negotiation for changes.
- **Risk Management:** The provider manages risk, but it may shift back to the client with changes.
- **IP Control:** Ownership transfers at project completion.

So, from the above explanations, we can consider that hiring dedicated developers offers several advantages over the fixed-price model, especially for long-term & complex projects. With a dedicated team, businesses can adjust the team size, project scope, and timelines based on evolving needs, which is crucial in dynamic markets. This model allows for higher quality output as the team is deeply involved, gaining a thorough understanding of the project and leading to a more customized and reliable product.

## Things to Consider Before Hiring a Dedicated Development Team

### Project Scope

Firstly, establish and detail all the prerequisites and characteristics of your project before searching for a group of individuals to work with.

### Budget

Ensure your total budget before hiring a team for a longer duration, as costs are ongoing.

### Skill Requirements

Seek a team that has worked with the particular programming language or technology required by your project. This is in addition to the fact that different skills can bring new ideas on board.

### Communicative Fit

Look for a team that understands your communication style and way of working. Assess how proficient the members are in expressing their thoughts and how they share information.

### Team Composition

Ensure the dedicated team you hire has individuals with varied responsibilities, such as software developers, designers, project managers, and quality assurances.

### Speed of Delivery

Ensure the beneficial turnaround time of the product before finalizing a dedicated team.

## Structure of a Dedicated Development Team

The type and complexity of your product, timeline, and the allocated budget determine the structure of a dedicated development team for hire. A typical dedicated development team must include the following roles:

### Project Manager

A project manager is the prime person responsible for the day-to-day management of the project.

### Business Analysts

They look after the business goals and values that bridge between the business and technical teams. They are instrumental in planning development activities and validating requirements to ensure alignment with business goals.

### Software Developers

A dedicated backend development team should be in charge of building and maintaining the inner mechanisms of your software. Frontend developers are mainly concerned with developing user-interacting parts and mobile apps to make the software more simplified.

### Software Architect

Moderates the technical details of a project and simultaneously carries out strategic-level planning. They choose the technology stack and make sure the solution you get is workable and optimized.

### QA Engineers

Quality assurance engineers undertake full responsibility for all the different parts of your software, be it code or any designs to make sure it achieves the standard.

### DevOps Engineers

DevOps engineers look after the development, testing, and automation processes. They facilitate continuous integration, deployment and enable interaction between the dev and ops teams during development for shorter development times and better quality.

## FAQs About Dedicated Development Teams

### 1. Why hire dedicated developers?

Hiring a dedicated developer can be proficient when there's a need for a quick turnaround on a project. They provide flexibility, cost-effectiveness, and focused expertise for long-term projects.

### 2. How much does it cost to hire full-stack developers in 2024?

The average full-stack developer costs around $20 to $60 hourly. For a dedicated development team, costs typically range from $5,000 to $25,000 per month depending on the team size and location.

### 3. What is the difference between a dedicated team and a fixed-price model?

A dedicated team offers flexibility, scalability, and continuous involvement, making it ideal for long-term projects with evolving requirements. A fixed-price model works best for projects with well-defined scope and strict budgets.

### 4. How long does it take to set up a dedicated development team?

Typically, it takes 1-2 weeks to set up a dedicated development team, including team selection, onboarding, and initial project setup. The exact timeline depends on your specific requirements and the provider's resources.

### 5. Can I scale the team up or down during the project?

Yes, one of the key advantages of a dedicated team model is scalability. You can add or remove team members based on project needs, making it ideal for projects with changing requirements.

### 6. What roles are typically included in a dedicated development team?

A typical dedicated team includes project managers, business analysts, software developers (frontend and backend), software architects, QA engineers, and DevOps engineers. The exact composition depends on your project needs.
