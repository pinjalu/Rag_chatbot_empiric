## What is IT Staff Augmentation?

IT staff augmentation is a flexible outsourcing strategy that allows companies to hire tech talent (dedicated developer or a team) globally and manage augmented teams directly. Instead of investing time and resources in the lengthy recruitment process, businesses can fill gaps in their IT workforce quickly and efficiently. This approach provides companies with the ability to scale their teams up or down based on current project needs without the long-term commitment and overhead costs associated with full-time hires.
