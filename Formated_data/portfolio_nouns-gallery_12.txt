## Project Details

- **Location:** France
- **Industry:** Non-Fungible Tokens
- **Platform:** Web Application
- **Team:** 2 Members

---

## Project Background

Noundry Gallery is a DAOApp where the traits for NFTs are made by their own users and community members. It offers services for users, to upload, manage, and edit their Traits, and Profile and make a really good network around the world in the Nouns community.

---

## Business Needs

The client wants to develop a platform where pixel artists showcase their nounish traits(Pixelated NFT) as 32x32 pixel images as transparent PNGs, style them using existing NounsDAO assets, and share them with the community.

It should also manage the Traits in the Gallery to showcase their NFTs and their Community profile.

## Project Challenges

### Trait Generation and Management

Generating and managing traits and NFTs posed a primary challenge during Nouns Gallery's development.

### Real-Time NFT Trait Integration

Integrating real-time NFT traits from sources around the world was a complex task.

### Data Security

Ensuring the security of the data, especially given the sensitive nature of NFTs, was a critical concern.

### User-Friendly Experience

Delivering a user-friendly experience to a large user base was a significant challenge.

### NFT Generation from Traits

The process of generating NFTs from the submitted traits using base traits needed to be implemented effectively.

### Cross-Device Compatibility

Creating a website that is accessible and usable on various devices, including phones, tablets, and laptops, required careful consideration and development efforts.

---

## Strategy and Solutions

### Random NFT generation

Nouns Gallery generates stunning NFTs from the base traits for community members to publish their traits in the gallery. Community members can also edit their traits.

### Community-driven traits

This is the most important part of the community since the NFT traits are made by a lot of individuals around the world. So making sure that they can interact with the site without any problem was one of the important things to keep in mind.
