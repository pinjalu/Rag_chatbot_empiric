Project Background

Project Background

The "For Rent" is a platform where users can book luxury hotels, resorts, and catering facilities. Additionally, it allows users to select catering options tailored to their preferences and dietary requirements, as well as reserve various games and activities for an enjoyable stay.

Business Needs

Business Needs

The "For Rent" project aims to address several key business needs. First, it is crucial to efficiently calculate and manage rental costs for customers staying at our hotel, using our catering services, and booking games or activities.

This will ensure accurate billing and customer satisfaction. Additionally, streamlining the rental process will improve our operational efficiency and reduce errors in financial transactions.

The project also needs to provide a user-friendly interface for customers to easily understand and pay for the services they've used, enhancing their experience and fostering loyalty.

Project Challenges

Implementing Currency Conversion

implementing currency conversion throughout the entire website is a complex task, requiring real-time exchange rate updates and ensuring accurate calculations for international customers.

This challenge involves not only technical aspects but also legal and compliance considerations.

Booking Management

Managing the booking system for specific check-in and check-out dates can be tricky due to potential conflicts and availability constraints.

Coordinating various accommodations, catering, and game bookings to align with a customer's desired dates while avoiding double bookings or overcommitting resources is a significant operational challenge.

It necessitates a robust reservation system and efficient communication with all relevant departments to maintain a seamless customer experience.

Strategy and Solutions

Manage Stay

This feature allows the system to oversee and organize the booking of accommodations, including rooms, suites, or other lodging options, for customers based on their specific check-in and check-out dates.

Manage Catering

This feature enables the management of catering services, allowing customers to select and book dining options based on their preferences and dietary requirements.

Manage Games

This feature facilitates the organization and reservation of various games and activities that customers can enjoy during their stay, ensuring proper scheduling and availability.

User Bookings

Users can book stays, caterings, and games based on the number of people in their group and specific dates and times. This feature provides a user-friendly interface for customers to make reservations and ensures that all bookings are in accordance with the chosen date and time slots, avoiding conflicts and overbooking.
