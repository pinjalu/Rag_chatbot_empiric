Data Collection Specialist [6 months to 1 year experience]

We are looking for a detail-oriented Data Collection Specialist to help us gather and organize data from various sources like Apollo, Clutch, LinkedIn, webinars, meetups, events, and job portals. This role is essential for building a strong database to support our sales and marketing efforts.

Job Description:

Find and collect data from platforms like Apollo, Clutch, LinkedIn, and job portals.

Attend webinars, events, and meetups to gather company and contact information.

Sort and structure the data in spreadsheets or CRM software.

Ensure all data is accurate, complete, and up to date.

Look for new websites and tools to collect data faster and better.

Analyze data to find trends or opportunities for our business.

Make sure all data collection follows laws and ethical guidelines.

Use approved tools and methods for gathering information.

Requirement:

Graduate in any stream (Bachelor’s degree).

Knowledge of platforms like Apollo, Clutch, and LinkedIn.

Basic skills in Excel, Google Sheets, or similar tools.

Attention to detail and ability to handle data accurately.

Good research and organization skills.

(Optional) Familiarity with web scraping tools like Python, BeautifulSoup, or Selenium.
