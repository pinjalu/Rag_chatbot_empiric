Buying a property of their own is a dream for many. Finding properties to tick your every requirement box or finding a good seller is difficult. We must admit that traditional methods like billboards, newspaper & magazine ads, and television and radio commercials are quite irrelevant nowadays.

With most individuals now accessing services through their smartphones, real estate mobile app development has become crucial.

According to the National Association of Realtors, about

41% of recent buyers

are taking their first step towards real estate buying or selling by looking online, primarily through their cell phones. This data shows many companies want to focus on mobile app development for real estate businesses.

A recent study also reported that about

61% of real estate business owners

admit that modernization of their core technology can accommodate a more considerable change in their profit margins.

If we go by the above data, there's much work pending for the real estate mobile app development companies.

Market Analysis

The global real estate software market generated a revenue of USD 10,285.7 million in 2023 and is expected to reach

USD 21,766.9 million by 2030

. North America led the real estate software market with a share of over 34.08% in 2022.

Key competitors in the market include

Zillow

,

Realtor.com

Rapid urban growth has led to a rise in real estate mobile app development projects using smart solutions in recent years. Furthermore, adopting new software technologies, such as cloud and artificial intelligence, across several applications in the real estate market is also driving the factor.

Benefits of Real Estate Apps

Real estate mobile apps have transformed how consumers interact with buying or selling real estate, providing several benefits to users and industry professionals.

Easy Property Browsing

: Apps make it convenient for users to search for properties, saving time while providing essential information.

User Insights

: Apps allow businesses to track user activity, providing valuable insights into what clients are searching for.

Feedback Mechanisms

: Including options for user feedback helps developers understand user preferences and improve app features.

Personalized Notifications

: Sending notifications based on user preferences enhances engagement and usability.

Streamlined Transactions

: Users can quickly buy or rent properties without needing to visit each location.

Brand Representation

: A well-designed app showcases your brand identity effectively.

Commission Models

: Implementing a commission-based model can generate additional revenue for properties sold through the app.

Revenue Models for Real Estate Apps

Real estate apps can make money using various methods based on their target audience and business model characteristics. Here we have listed a few:

Subscription plans

App users can use a subscription plan to avail of top features, such as selective property listings. This strategy ensures a consistent revenue stream by giving the users benefits like advanced search filters and priority notifications.

In-App Purchases

Users can make one-time purchases for premium services like prominent listings or access to detailed market reports.

Allowing third-party ads on the App

Real estate ads, mortgage services, or home renovation offers can produce significant money, especially with a large user base.

Premium Listings

Charging property owners or brokers a fee to showcase their listings prominently in the app can increase visibility and speed up sales.

8 Must-have Features of a Successful Real Estate App

A good real estate app must offer several critical features to suit user needs and promote engagement.

1. User Profiles

Creating individual user profiles is vital in real estate mobile app development. They offer customized experiences like allowing users to save their checked preferences on the app and track property searches.

2. Property Listings

The property listings feature in a real estate mobile app will offer crucial details about a property, like the size, pricing, amenities, etc., in an organized manner.

3. Filtering Option

A filtering options feature will help the users filter out options beyond their purchase range or liking. With advanced search filters, customers can identify their requirements, such as location, price, and property type.

4. Interactive maps

Interactive maps for enabling the customers to view property locations and adjacent amenities.

5. Virtual Tours

360-degree virtual tours to allow customers to examine properties remotely.

6. Creative Visual

A native and visually appealing design to keep users interested and satisfied.

7. Cost Calculator

Provide a cost calculator feature to assist the users in considering the monetary aspects and total cost of property dealings.

8. In-app Assistance

In-app help options like chatbots and direct messaging ensure that users receive prompt assistance.
