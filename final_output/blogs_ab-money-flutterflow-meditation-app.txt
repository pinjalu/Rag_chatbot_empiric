Project Overview

Ab.Money is a free meditation and mindset app created by Alexandra Belliakova, also known as Sasha Belaire. It aims to help users improve their mental well-being through guided meditations and mindset exercises. The app is available on both the App Store and Google Play Store, offering content in English and Russian to cater to a diverse audience.

Development Journey

Initially developed using another no-code tool, Ab.Money faced scalability issues as its user base grew. To overcome these challenges and enhance the app's capabilities, the decision was made to switch to FlutterFlow. Within just two months, from February 2022 to March 2022, the app was successfully rebuilt and launched using FlutterFlow.

Key Features of AB Money App

Bilingual Support

Ab.Money offers unique features in both English and Russian, tailored to meet the needs of its diverse users.

Platform Availability

The app is available on both the

App Store

and

Google Play Store

, making it accessible to a wide range of users.

User-Centric Design

The app includes original meditations by Sasha Belaire, custom audio players, video courses for Russian users, daily tarot cards, affirmations, and multiple diaries for tracking emotions and goals.

The Decision to Switch to FlutterFlow

In 2021, Alexandra's team approached the development team to build Ab.Money. After releasing several versions with another tool and encountering scalability issues, they switched to FlutterFlow. This tool offered:

Rapid Development

: The team rebuilt the app in just two months.

Enhanced Functionality

: FlutterFlow provided custom functions, seamless integration with third-party software, and the ability to add unique features.

Scalability

: FlutterFlow's capabilities allowed the app to handle a larger user base effectively.

Implementation and Customization

The team leveraged FlutterFlow's native features to build 80-90% of Ab.Money's functionalities, using custom actions and widgets to create a unique user experience. Key features include:

Conditional Visibility

: Tailored content and features for specific user groups.

Custom Audio Player and Video Courses

: Enhanced media functionality for users.

Affirmations and Tarot Cards

: Daily motivational features using FlutterFlow's random data generator.

Diary Features

: Multiple diaries for users to document their progress and goals, with data managed through Firebase.

Results and Future Prospects

The app has achieved significant success, including:

High User Ratings

: A 4.8 rating on the App Store and a perfect 5.0 rating on the Google Play Store.

Widespread Use

: Over 250,000 users and counting.

Revenue

: Over $100,000 in client-reported revenue.

Top Ranking

: First place in the education category in the App Store for Eastern Europe.

Conclusion

Ab.Money's success story highlights the potential of FlutterFlow in developing high-quality, scalable apps quickly and efficiently. If you're looking to develop a mobile app, our
